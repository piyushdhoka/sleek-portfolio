# Twix_AI : X posts refining platform

## Overview

Twix AI is a modern platform designed to enhance X posts by leveraging AI to improve structure, conciseness, and engagement, providing users with a seamless experience to craft impactful posts.

## What Users Can Do

- **Refine posts:**  Use AI-powered suggestions to enhance the clarity and impact of X posts..
- **Customize Content::**  Tailor posts with structured formatting and concise language.
- **Track Usage:** Monitor API usage and credit management for efficient post creation.
- **Responsive Design:**  Enjoy a modern, device-friendly interface for a smooth experience across platforms.m  

## Why i built this

I built this platform to solve a fundamental issue i faced while studying as follows -

- I noticed when people use ChatGPT, their posts often look too AI-generated and lack a human touch
